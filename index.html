Homemade Middle Eastern Jelly & Custard Recipe

Vanilla Custard with Strawberry Jelly and Bananas

كاسترد الفانيليا مع جيلي الفراولة والموز

By: Shams Hasan بواسطة : شمس حسن

Servings

الحصص

Family size: up to 24 regular square-sized portions الحجم العائلي : حتى 24 قطعة بحجم مربع عادي

Prep time

وقت التحضير

15-20 minutes ١٥ - ٢٠ دقيقة

Ingredients Measurements
cardamom spice 1/4 teaspoon
any jelly fruit flavor of your choosing 1 packet
custard powder 2 tablespoons
milk 1 cup
sugar 1-2 teaspoons
ulker biscuits/soft biscuits/cookies of your choosing 1 pack minimum
pyrex container medium/large size
optional: chocolate chips As much as you please
optional: fruit(s) 1-3 fruits

Directions:

Jelly:

  1. Boil .75 mL of water
  2. Once boiled, pour .50 mL into a medium-sized bowl
  3. Add in your jelly packet, the remaining water, and mix
  4. Set aside for 5 minutes

Custard:

  1. Mix the full amount of milk, custard, and cardamom in a bowl until your custard is fully dissolved.
  2. Pour your mix into a small pot and place on medium-heat stove
  3. Whisk until mixture is thickened

Next,

  1. Place a layer of biscuits/cookies onto your pyrex container
  2. Add on your custard mix and let it cool for 10 minutes
  3. Now add on your jelly mix slowly and in a neat fashion
  4. Top it off with the sweets of your choice
  5. Refrigerate for a minimum of 3 hours
